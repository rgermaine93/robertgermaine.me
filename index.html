Sports Technology

Statellite is a sports performance and recruiting platform for athletes, coaches, and recruiters. It helps athletes track results, measure progress, and build shareable profiles while giving coaches a faster way to manage testing and analyze performance. At the core is NOVA, a scoring system that turns raw data into more meaningful benchmarks based on age, sex, and location.

Caisely

Legal Technology

Caisely is a legal intake and case management platform built to help personal injury firms capture leads, qualify them faster, and move every opportunity toward a clear outcome. It brings lead sources, intake forms, follow-up automation, communication, and decisioning into one system so firms can reduce lead leakage, improve speed-to-lead, and keep their pipeline organized.
